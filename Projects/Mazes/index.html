Mazes

Intro

About a month ago I had a student come to me with a seemingly simple assignment of creating a maze. There were few constraints but in this task I was required to create a maze that simply had a start in one corner and an end in the opposite with walls in between. "Okay no problem" I thought.. but then there was this part b. and a part c as well, and then some extra credit questions like adding on constraints like.. no loops, no dead ends, and so on as bonus questions. Interested in such an open ended yet simple task, I figured I'd give it a go myself with as many variants as I could imagine and program. This is the result.

Data

Binary Walls

This may seem a bit blocky, but it's perhaps the simplest way I can imagine thinking of space. There either is a wall (black) or there isn't (white). Easy to render in a console.
